About


Taekwon-Do is a Korean martial art, an effective art of self defence as well as a competitive sport. Taekwon-Do society was established in 2013 by Mr. Teo Khoon Teng on TAR universities college.

We are using "zero" to be our society logo, this is because we are start from begin, we are start from nothing, but we have the change to beyond everyone. "Zero" is also mean we come together, like a familly.

Taekwondo society's aim is to provide an environment to self defence with physical, mental and spiritual training. Our society also help to bodybuilding, stamina, flexibility.
